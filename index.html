About Us

We have been saving time, nerves and money for industrial companies since 2007, using the experience of a professional team of forwarders, delivering goods anywhere in the world. ABIPA specializes in one of the most strategically important areas of the logistics industry — the Russian and Baltic markets. Central offices in Lithuania (Vilnius) and Russia (Moscow, Vladivostok), representative offices in Latvia, Estonia, the Republic of Belarus allow you to make the most of the opportunities of the largest international hub. Our clients choose air, auto, container sea and rail transportation, groupage cargo, customs brokerage services and foreign trade outsourcing, which are optimal for their business at a particular time. Autotransportation is a simple and well-known mode of transport by land not involving railway. As industrialization advanced, freight-forwarding market substantially simplified; modern types of road transport allow delivering fragile, heavy, and oversized cargoes, as well as shipping goods in bulk. Road transport is most commonly used to deliver cargoes to highways.

Transporting goods by sea is one of the oldest logistics concepts in the world; it is optimal in terms of price-quality ratio. Modern sea transportation is versatile and allowing to make the delivery of multimodal cargoes a cost-effective process. Our experienced specialist will find an optimal solution on sea shipping.

JSC Transimeksa is one of Lithuania’s largest transportation companies. Through close cooperation with other companies in the group, Transimeksa is able to offer all logistics services. This includes cargo transportation by personal and partner transport (with sea and air transportation), customs intermediary services, and warehousing in a personal cargo terminal. The Transimeksa team is always ready to provide you with the information you’re looking for and help you to find innovative, efficient solutions that you can rely on.

The code of ethics is a set of principles and values that underpin the TRANSIMEKSA group of companies. Our principles and values establish the expectations and standards of behavior for our employees, suppliers, partners, and clients. Sometimes in everyday activities we may question if a certain behavior is appropriate. The principles provided in the ethical code indicate how the company’s values should be implemented in practice and what appropriate behavior looks like. We are a member of the United Nations Global Compact. It is a global initiative that unites companies aiming to adopt sustainable and socially responsible business strategies, activities, and ethics. This pact is based on 10 main principles, including anti-corruption, environmental protection, human rights, and fair labor.

Our primary service proposition is the transportation of FTLs, LTLs and small cargo loads by road. With our experienced drivers, modern truck and trailer fleet, and reliable branch and partner network, we guarantee the smooth transportation of goods across Europe and the CIS.
